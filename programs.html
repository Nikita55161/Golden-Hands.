Our Programs

Evidence-informed, community-driven programs designed to meet people where they are.

Schools for Wellness

In-school workshops on emotional intelligence, stress management, and peer support for secondary students. Currently running in 12 schools across Nairobi and surrounding counties.

Healing Circles

Weekly facilitated peer support sessions for adults dealing with depression, anxiety, grief, and relationship trauma. Safe, confidential spaces to share, listen, and heal together.

Digital Wellness Hub

Online resources, self-help tools, and virtual counselling access for those unable to attend in person. Expanding to reach Kenyans in remote areas.

Clinician & Community Training

Capacity-building for community health workers, nurses, and village elders on mental health first aid.

Rural Outreach

Mobile teams travelling to underserved counties to deliver screenings, community talks, and referrals across Nairobi, Kisumu, Mombasa, Nakuru, Eldoret, and Meru.
