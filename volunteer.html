Volunteer With Us

Be the change. Join a team that is making mental health matter in Kenya.

Community Facilitator

Lead support group sessions and community education events. Full training provided. Ideal for people with backgrounds in counselling, social work, or anyone with a compassionate heart.

Digital Volunteer

Help manage our social media, website, and digital wellness content. We need graphic designers, content writers, videographers, and social media managers.

Outreach Volunteer

Join our mobile teams on county-level outreach trips — typically weekends, 1–2 times per month. No prior experience required.
