About Us

Our Mission

Golden Hands CBO is dedicated to making mental health support accessible, affordable, and stigma-free for all Kenyans — especially those in underserved and rural communities.

Our History

Founded in 2025 by a group of mental health advocates, social workers, and community leaders. From humble beginnings in Nairobi, we have grown into an organisation touching lives across 6 counties in Kenya.

The Issues We Address

We tackle depression, anxiety, trauma, PTSD, student mental health, and the deep-rooted stigma that prevents people from seeking help. Cultural and societal barriers remain major obstacles — our work challenges these every day.

Our Values

Compassion — We meet people with empathy and without judgment.

Dignity — Every person deserves care and respect.

Community — Healing happens together.

Inclusivity — We serve everyone, regardless of background.

Accountability — We are transparent with our communities and donors.
