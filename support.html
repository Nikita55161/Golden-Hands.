Your donation directly funds mental health support for Kenyans who cannot afford it.

Why Your Support Matters

Mental health services in Kenya are severely underfunded. Over 75% of people experiencing mental health conditions receive no treatment. Your contribution helps us close this gap.

What Your Donation Does

KSh 500 — Funds one peer support group session

KSh 1,500 — Covers counselling for one person

KSh 5,000 — Supports a full school wellness workshop

KSh 10,000 — Funds one rural outreach trip
